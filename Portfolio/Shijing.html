Shijing

Glasses Virtual Try-on

Scenting the potential business opportunity in the huge Chinese glasses market, I initiated to build an e-commerce platform where customers could virtually try glasses on, buy products with high performance cost and enjoy reliable service. Well understanding customers' concerns about online glasses shopping, I led my team to design our value proposition and business model, and successfully validated them in the market. Then we developed our product Shijing 1.0 with AR technology. Designers and engineers were working closely within my team to continually and effectively complete iterations and better our product.
