Buydar

Future Retail Experience

Buydar, a mobile app which allows users to easily search products they see in brick-and-mortar stores, helps with purchase decision-making through price comparison and user reviews, and enhances shopping efficiency via mobile payment and self-checkout. Also, Buydar meets small retailers’ needs by digitizing offline shopping. The shopping data collected empowers them to perform precision marketing, inventory optimization and cost reduction.

Buydar is an example of my exploration in future retail experience design. In the future, e-commerce and brick-and-mortar stores are no longer binary. The boundary would be ambiguous. Customer, product and environment are going to mingle and create new value together. And technologies like IoT, AI, AR, etc. provide ample opportunities to do so.
